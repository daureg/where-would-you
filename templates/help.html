Well, I kind of lied to you. Before you start, I want to give you a few more explanations to save you time later.

First, after reading the question at the top like , you can either it or answer it.

To do so, use the tools on the top left to draw circles or polygons on the city map. {# #}

Then you can choose as many locations as you want

Finally, you have three options:

  1. add up to 2 more areas in the same manner.
  2. click the edit button to move previous areas, change their size or delete them (by dragging them outside the city). {# #}
  3. move to the next question, by clicking the Done button and telling at which time you would do this activity. {# #}

Sometimes, you may be thinking of a place without remembering its address. Thus you can look up its name on the top right box and click it to center the map on it (you still have to draw a shape though, as surroundings are more informative than single locations).

Now you can really start, I swear.
